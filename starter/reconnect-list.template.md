## Warmth scale
- **Hot** — worked together closely; would vouch for you unprompted.
- **Warm** — worked together; would reply and help if asked.
- **Cool** — know of you; needs a reason to re-engage.

## Rules
- Rank by (how warm × how useful), not by how impressive the company is.
- Never blast. One genuine, specific message per person.
- Track replies honestly. A "no reply" after two touches is a no — move on without resentment.
