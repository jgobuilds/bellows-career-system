## Conventions (the system reads these)
### Capitalization
- **Capitalize** when it's a name or label: section headers, skills-list items, product names.
- **Lowercase** when it's a generic concept in prose: "product marketing," "positioning," "pricing."
- ATS matching is case-insensitive — no benefit to mimicking a posting's capitalization.

### Never do this
- No invented numbers. The 31%→45% signup lift is real and I can explain how it was measured.
- No scale overreach. I'm an individual contributor; I've never managed anyone. Never imply otherwise.
- No borrowed seniority. I *supported* analyst briefings; I never owned an AR program. Say it that way.
- The Google Analytics cert is expired. It's labeled prior, always.
- Never claim demand gen, paid media, or brand. If the job needs it, it's the wrong job.
