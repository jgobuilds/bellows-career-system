# Starter — blanks + a worked example (Maya Okonkwo, fictional)

This folder holds two things for each artifact: a blank **`.template`** to copy into `personal/`,
and a filled-in **`.example`** so you can see what "good" looks like before you face an empty file.
A blank template is where most people quit — read the examples first.

**The example person is deliberately not in data.** Maya is a mid-career product marketer (a PMM reaching
for Senior). The system works for any field — your lane just has to be described honestly.

| Read this example | What to look at |
|---|---|
| `career-profile.example.md` | How specific a *finished* profile is. Real numbers, named tools, and a blunt **"What you are NOT"** section. |
| `pipeline.example.md` | Honest scoring: an 8 worth chasing, a caveated 6, and a 4 that says **skip this** out loud. |
| `writing-style.example.md` | Voice profile. The **samples** at the bottom do more work than the rules at the top. |
| `userconfig.template.py` | The settings you edit. Note the **hard gates** — encoding what you *aren't* is what saves you time (see lesson 4). |

## The four things worth stealing

1. **Write "What you are NOT" first.** Maya's profile says plainly: not demand gen, not brand, not a data
   scientist. That single section is why the system tells her to *skip* the Brand & Growth Marketing Manager
   role instead of helping her write a resume that overclaims.

2. **Let the scores be unflattering.** Her board has a 4 on it, clearly labeled "skip this." If every role
   scored an 8, the number would mean nothing and she'd stop reading it.

3. **Route the good one through a person.** The 8 is marked WARM — reach Lena before applying. An 8 with a
   referral beats a 10 sent cold, and that's the whole ballgame.

4. **Make sure your hard gates actually fire.** Maya's gate started as `"brand marketing"` — which does NOT
   match a job titled *"Brand & Growth Marketing Manager"*. It had to become `"brand"`. Run one sweep and
   read the Drops: a gate that never fires is doing nothing for you.
